WHICH FINANCE CATEGORY IS RIGHT FOR ME?

Credit, mortgage and insurance broking

More than half of Australian mortgages are written by a broker, and that number is rising. Brokers play and essential role in Australia’s unique credit market.

Stock broking

Stockbroking and trading offers a fast-paced career trading stocks, currencies or bonds on behalf of clients. There can be a heavy sales component to many trading jobs as you work with clients to invest in certain portfolios.

Finance

Careers in finance focus on the efficient management of money; whether that be saving, borrowing or investing, you'll be researching and analysing the market to forecast future market movements.

Banking

Banking careers provide wide ranging opportunities to work with individual customers, large corporate institutions or the government to address all their banking needs.

Accounting

No business - big or small succeeds without the assistance of a quality accountant, and that goes way past simply crunching the numbers.
